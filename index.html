Console Commands:


 

world('command', x) - Commands that affect the entire environment.


    ~ 'status' : Reports the current state of the world map.
    ~ 'new map' : Resizes the map by the value of X (e.g. if X is 20, the map will resize to 20x20).
    ~ 'spawn obstacle' : Spawn X amount obstacles in random locations for rovers to find within the current map.
      Running this too many times will likely keep the rovers from being able to go anywhere. They'll basically be buried.
    ~ 'spawn rover' : Spawns X amount of rovers up to a maximum of 6 on the map.

 

sendTo('rover name', command) - Selects a specific rover , if any, and provides it a command or sequence of commands to follow.


    ~ 'Rx' : Rotates selected rover to the right x amount of times.
    ~ 'Lx' : Rotates selected rover to the left x amount of times.
    ~ 'Fx' : Selected rover attempts to move forward by x amount of spaces.
    ~ 'Bx' : Selected rover attempts to move backward by x amount of spaces.

Note: All rovers face North by Default.
